About Us

Located within Asaba Airport, the VIP Lounge offers a serene environment where you can relax while waiting for your flight. Our lounge is not just about comfort, it also features ambient lighting and dish channel viewing, enhancing your experience. We specialize in providing a selection of premium snacks and drinks in a comfortable and exclusive setting designed for travelers seeking a quiet escape with all the amenities needed to refresh and recharge.

Our Menu

Our lounge menu is thoughtfully curated to provide guests with a variety of snack options and a wide range of drinks, from coffees to fine wines. Our offerings are perfect for a quick refreshment or a relaxing break in your journey.
